Company Overview

CivicSpan IT Group is a Virginia-based technology consulting and digital infrastructure support company specializing in engineering technology environments, technical support operations, and infrastructure-focused business solutions.

We provide responsive and scalable support services for organizations operating in transportation, engineering, infrastructure, and government-adjacent sectors. Our expertise includes Bentley ecosystem support, ProjectWise administration assistance, onboarding workflows, technical troubleshooting, systems support, and digital infrastructure services.

CivicSpan bridges operational technology support with modern business technology solutions to help organizations improve workflow continuity, user accessibility, and technical efficiency.
