This Cookie Policy explains how CivicSpan IT Group uses cookies and similar technologies when visitors access this website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on a user’s device when visiting a website. Cookies help websites function properly, improve performance, remember preferences, and provide analytics information.

How We Use Cookies

CivicSpan IT Group may use cookies and related technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Analyze website traffic and visitor behavior
  • Understand how users interact with website content
  • Support website security and reliability
  • Enhance overall user experience

Analytics Cookies

This website may use third-party analytics services, including Google Analytics, to collect anonymized traffic and usage information. These services may use cookies to measure visitor interactions, page views, session activity, and general website performance.

Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services connected to this website may place cookies on user devices for analytics, security, or functionality purposes. CivicSpan IT Group does not directly control third-party cookies.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow users to manage, disable, or delete cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features or functionality.

Cookie Consent

By continuing to use this website, users acknowledge and agree to the use of cookies and similar technologies as described in this Cookie Policy.
